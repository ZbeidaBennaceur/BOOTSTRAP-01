OUR SIGNATURE DISHES

Sushis

Our sushi is crafted with care, using perfectly seasoned vinegared rice and high-quality fresh fish. Each piece is a true delight, combining the subtle sweetness of the rice with the freshness of the fish for an authentic tasting experience.

PRICE: 25 TND

Tempura

Our tempura features lightly battered and crispy-fried seafood and vegetables, providing a perfect balance of texture and flavor. The delicate crunch of the batter complements the tender ingredients, creating a satisfying and flavorful dish.

PRICE: 45 TND

Mochis

Mochi is a sweet treat made from glutinous rice, with a soft and chewy texture. They are filled with various flavors such as red bean paste or ice cream, offering a refreshing and delicious Japanese dessert. Each dish is a tribute to authentic Japanese culinary traditions, prepared with fresh ingredients and expert craftsmanship.

PRICE: 15 TND

OUR PHILOSOPHY

At Sakura no Hana, we welcome you like family. Born from a deep-rooted passion for traditional Japanese cuisine, our restaurant is not just a place to eat, but a space where the warmth of family and the comfort of home meet. The recipes we serve have been passed down through generations, bringing with them a sense of history and love for the art of Japanese cooking. Our story is one of tradition, care, and shared memories. Growing up in a family that cherished authentic flavors and the joy of coming together around a meal, we’ve dedicated ourselves to offering you the same sense of comfort and familiarity. From the first bite of our perfectly crafted sushi to the rich flavors of our ramen, each dish is prepared with the utmost care and the freshest ingredients, just as we’ve always done at home. At Sakura no Hana, we want you to feel like you're dining with us at our family table, where every meal is an expression of love, tradition, and the joy of sharing good food with those you care about. Join us and experience the taste of home, where every dish is made with love, and every visit feels like a warm embrace.
